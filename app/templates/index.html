About CrypticNotes

CrypticNotes is a secure notes taking web app that offers users a safe and reliable place to store their notes online. Security is a top priority for CrypticNotes, which is why each note can be encrypted with an encryption algorithm, ensuring that their information remains private and protected. In addition to encryption, CrypticNotes also offers two-factor authentication (2FA) with email OTP authentication, adding an extra layer of security to the app. Furthermore, users can extract text from images and handwriting, making it easier to digitize and organize their notes. CrypticNotes aims to provide a secure and user- friendly experience for those looking to keep their notes safe and accessible.
